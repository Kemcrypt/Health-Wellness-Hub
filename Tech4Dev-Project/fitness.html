Simple ways to stay fit

Exercise

Regular physical activity, such as running, walking, or swimming, is crucial for maintaining good physical health. Aim for at least 30 minutes most days of the week.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water throughout the day to keep your body functioning optimally.

Eat a balanced diet

Focus on plenty of fruits, vegetables, and whole grains. Choose lean protein sources like fish, beans, and poultry. Limit processed foods, sugary drinks, and unhealthy fats.

Get enough sleep
Aim for 7-8 hours of sleep each night. Sleep deprivation can negatively impact your hormones and lead to weight gain.
